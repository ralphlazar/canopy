Nkosi Sikelel' iAfrika / Mungu ibariki Afrika“God bless Africa” - the opening words of Nkosi Sikelel' iAfrika, written in Zulu/Xhosa in 1897 and now part of five national anthems across the continent. Mungu ibariki Afrika is the same prayer in Swahili.
