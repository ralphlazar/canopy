About
Canopy is a public resource on conservation finance in Africa.

What it covers

  • 148 tracked projects across 54 countries - methodology, certification, and current status
  • 22 funding instruments explained - from voluntary carbon credits to debt-for-nature swaps
  • Article 6.2 readiness mapped for every African nation
  • Weekly intelligence across carbon markets, biodiversity policy, conservation finance, and philanthropic flows

Who it is for

  • Funders and family offices allocating capital to African conservation
  • Investors who need to understand the landscape before committing
  • Advisors who need to brief clients quickly
  • Anyone tracking what is actually happening at the frontier of conservation finance

The problem it solves

The information infrastructure for African conservation finance is fragmented. Data sits across registries, government portals, and specialist publications. Getting a clear picture requires weeks of research most investors don't have time for. Canopy puts it in one place, kept current, explained clearly.

Independence

  • No advertising
  • No sponsored content
  • No commercial relationship with any project developer or registry
  • Project scorecards - coming in Phase 2 - will be independently rated
